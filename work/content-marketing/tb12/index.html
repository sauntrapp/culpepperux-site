Tom Brady's TB12 Sports

One brand, every channel: a year of strategy, editorial, and video to take TB12 from elite athletes to everyone.

ROLE Copy and Content Writer, Brand Strategist, Video Producer
BRIEF Take the training regimen of the NFL's top quarterback and create a lifestyle fitness brand that helps athletes prevent sports injuries and achieve peak performance.
EXECUTION Create, execute and manage an editorial strategy comprised of written and video content to democratize the brand and explain why TB12 isn't just for elite athletes.
01

Editorial Strategy and Content Writing

In addition to planning and writing a year-long editorial strategy for TB12's mobile app and website, I wrote and managed a four-person team of freelancers.

02

Video Producing

As interviewer and producer, I spoke with former Green Beret and TB12 client Kevin Flike to explore his story of recovery from combat injuries sustained during his deployment in Afghanistan.

03

Video Scripting

In addition to producing and writing stories about TB12, my responsibilities included researching the methodology of Tom Brady's sports regimen and translating its essence into easy to understand video scripts for the video production team to shoot.

04

Brand Strategy and Digital Campaign

The “Peak Performer” campaign was a program I developed to help shift the appeal of TB12 from an elite “athletes only” brand to a democratized and accessible platform for people of all athletic abilities. In addition to interviewing and crafting narratives about the personal challenges clients sought to overcome, we created a digital community for them to interact, engage and share with one another.

Onboarding for KAYAK's "Trips" feature.

WHAT

From flights to dinner reservations, Trips is the all-in-one platform to manage your itinerary—even if you booked it somewhere else.

CHALLENGE

My task was to distill the most important elements of this feature into an easy to follow desktop sequence for users to reach their first step in the onboarding journey: Syncing their emails to automatically add bookings to their Trips dashboard.
